
### Form 10-K
Publicly-traded companies must register Form 10-K with SEC. Provides a comprehensive look at the companies financial health for the throughout the year.


### Questions
- Is there value in mining 10-K form for a company? 
- Could sentiment be looked at but also running statistical tests on the company debt, revenue, plans, balance sheet, etc.

### Challenges
- Since a 10-K is backwards looking report (covering the previous fiscal year), how can this information be incorporated into a sentiment / statistical model?
