
Uses a Lexicon (also known as a dictionary)

Contains associations of some words / phrases to positive sentiment, and other words to negative sentiment

Sentiment score is determined based on the frequency of the context of these events

### Pros

- Fast, easy to process text and calculate a score quickly
- Able to reverse engineer a sentiment score by looking up the associated sentiment weights

### Cons

- Can struggle with sarcasm, negation, idiomatic phrases. Some text sources (Reddit, twitter, etc) may have their own colloquial phrases or "hidden" meaning - "Diamond hands"

For financial text, most widely used lexicon is the Loughran-McDonald (LM) dataset
